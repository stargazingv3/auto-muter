Welcome to Auto Muter

Before we start, please review our data handling practices.

What the extension does: Auto Muter identifies when a specific person is speaking in a browser tab and automatically mutes it.

What data is collected:

  • To enroll a speaker, you provide a YouTube URL. We download the audio, create a mathematical "voice print," and then delete the original audio.
  • When active, the extension sends short audio snippets from the tab to our server for analysis. These are **never stored** and are deleted immediately.

By clicking "Agree," you consent to this data processing. For full details, please read our Privacy Policy.
